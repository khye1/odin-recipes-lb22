Odin Project 2022

Description: Pasta with tomato sauce is one of the heartiest and most heartwarming meals in any home cook's repertoire. It's perfect for serving a crowd or for whipping up on a quiet evening home with a loved one. But perhaps one of the best parts about pasta with tomato sauce is how many different tasty forms the sauce can take. Here, we break down the most common red sauce variations and show how to add them to your dinner rotation.

Ingredients

  1. 4 slices bacon, diced
    2. ½ cup chopped onion 1 teaspoon minced garlic ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes 2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes 1 pound linguine pasta, uncooked 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
